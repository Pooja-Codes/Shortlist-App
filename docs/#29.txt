title #29 (Students&Parents) Categorize High Schools and Make Lists


Students/Parents->ShortLists/UI: Drag high schools they like to a list
ShortLists/UI -> DataBase: Retrieve the information from the database
DataBase --> ShortLists/UI: Save the school's info in the lists
Students/Parents->ShortLists/UI: Students can change the name, color, and fonts of the lists
